Business events are now automatically stored and queryable in Eventhouse, enabling KQL analysis and Real-Time Dashboard creation directly from operational event streams. This preview feature connects business process events to analytics pipelines without requiring custom ingestion code, making event-driven analytics accessible to a wider audience.
