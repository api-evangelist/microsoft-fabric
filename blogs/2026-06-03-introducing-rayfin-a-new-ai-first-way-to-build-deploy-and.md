Rayfin is a new AI-first platform for building, deploying, and governing application backends that keeps solutions secure, compliant, and governed from the ground up. It integrates with the Fabric ecosystem to provide application developers with a managed backend that automatically enforces data policies and compliance requirements without manual configuration.
