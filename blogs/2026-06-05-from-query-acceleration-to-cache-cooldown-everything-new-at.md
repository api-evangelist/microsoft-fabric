Graph in Fabric is now generally available, enabling organizations to model, visualize, and analyze complex relationships at scale and allowing deterministic reasoning over paths within their data. This capability integrates graph database semantics directly into the Fabric platform, enabling relationship-based analytics alongside traditional tabular and time-series workloads.
