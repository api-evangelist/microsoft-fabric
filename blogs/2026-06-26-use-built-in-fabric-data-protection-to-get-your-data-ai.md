Every organization wants to put AI to work on its data. Copilot, agents, and generative AI promise faster insight , but they raise a hard question for security teams: If AI can reach all our data, what stops it from surfacing the wrong data to the wrong person? The answer is to make your data AI-ready — classified, access-controlled, protected, and observable.
