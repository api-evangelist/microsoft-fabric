A new built-in stored procedure for the SQL analytics endpoint in Microsoft Fabric; sp_get_table_health_metric. This feature gives you a T-SQL-native way to check the structural health of your Lakehouse tables, so you can make informed decisions about table maintenance before it impacts your users. The challenge: Blind maintenance or reactive firefighting If you use Lakehouse tables through the SQL analytics endpoint, you may notice queries gradually slowing down.
