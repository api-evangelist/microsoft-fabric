Activator now publishes Business Events, converting KPI alerts, threshold breaches, and query results into structured, governed signals that can trigger downstream actions across the Fabric platform. This preview capability bridges the gap between analytics insights and operational responses, enabling automated workflows based on real-time data conditions.
