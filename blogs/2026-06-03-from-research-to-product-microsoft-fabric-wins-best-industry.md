The CoddSpeed research behind Microsoft Fabric's GPU-accelerated query processing won the Best Industry Paper Award at SIGMOD 2026, recognizing the technical innovation in high-performance analytics. The paper details the journey from academic research to a production system now powering the GPU-accelerated Fabric Data Warehouse early access preview.
