Microsoft Fabric introduces a GPU-accelerated execution engine for Data Warehouse in early access preview, addressing performance challenges in modern analytics workloads that require processing massive datasets at high concurrency. This new engine, built on the CoddSpeed research that won the Best Industry Paper Award at SIGMOD 2026, delivers order-of-magnitude query performance improvements for complex analytical workloads.
