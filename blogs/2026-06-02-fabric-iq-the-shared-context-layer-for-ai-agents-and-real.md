Fabric IQ enables people and AI agents to operate together in real time, sharing an always-current understanding of the state of the business, allowing both humans and autonomous systems to reason over the same governed data context. This shared context layer addresses the challenge of keeping AI agents synchronized with live business data without manual refresh cycles.
