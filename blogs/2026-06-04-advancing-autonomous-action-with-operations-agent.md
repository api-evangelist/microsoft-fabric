The operations agent moves teams from dashboards-and-alerts to outcomes by automating monitoring, diagnosis, and action in Microsoft Fabric, now generally available. The agent proactively identifies anomalies, diagnoses root causes, and executes remediation steps without requiring human intervention for routine operational issues.
