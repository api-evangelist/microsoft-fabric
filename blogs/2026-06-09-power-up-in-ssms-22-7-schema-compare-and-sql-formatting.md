With SSMS 22.7, we're expanding that foundation with two capabilities that database professionals have been asking for in SSMS: graphical schema compare and a built-in SQL formatter. In addition to those key features, we're continuing to expand the SQL projects support in the Database DevOps (Preview) with SQLCMD variable support in the publish dialog.
